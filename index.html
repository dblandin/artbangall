About

artBangall is a pop-up art exhibition set in the backdrop of a vacant former restaurant in the hamlet of Bangall, NY. A playful riff on today’s contemporary art fairs, the show will feature the work of thirty-five emerging artists working in painting, drawing, sculpture, and photography in an unconventional space. artBangall’s intention is to make use of and revitalize the Hudson Valley vacant storefront with a vibrant and diverse art show. The show is curated by two artists, Amanda Brown and Stephanie Del Carpio, both based in New York City. The opening reception will be held on Saturday, June 2nd from 2-8pm. artBangall will be open to the public by appointment through June 16th.
